# EasyContent - an AI content automation

> AI-powered viral video workflow: Research → Script → Edit, automated in under 6 minutes.

![EasyContent Dashboard](screenshots/02-dashboard.png)

## Overview

EasyContent automates the complete viral video creation process. What traditionally takes 5-6 hours (research, scripting, editing) now takes approximately 6 minutes through AI-powered automation.

## Features

- **AI Research**: Analyzes top creators and extracts winning video scripts
- **Script Generation**: Creates personalized scripts from proven viral templates
- **Automated Editing**: Processes raw footage into polished, platform-optimized content
- **Complete Workflow**: Seamless integration from research to final video

## How It Works

1. **Research**: AI analyzes viral content and extracts successful patterns
2. **Script**: Generate personalized scripts from proven templates
3. **Film**: Record your video following the script
4. **Edit**: Upload footage, get AI-edited result in <5 minutes

**Time Saved**: 97% reduction (6 hours → 6 minutes)

## Tech Stack

**Frontend & Interface:**
- Airtable (Database, Interfaces, Forms)
- Rocket.new Agent (Landing Page)
- Claude (Design & Ideation)

**Backend & Automation:**
- n8n (Workflow Automation)
- ChatGPT (Script Generation)
- Apify (Web Scraping)
- Google Cloud (Infrastructure)
- Dropbox (File Storage)

**Quick Start:**
1. Visit the [landing page](https://tinyurl.com/easycontent-rocketnew)
2. Click "Start Creating"
3. Follow the workflow: Research → Script → Film → Edit
